# Reverse Actualization Experiment: Safe Navigation (B1)

**Problem:** Design a system so boats never hit underwater rocks.
**Date:** 2026-05-07
**Model:** Seed-2.0-mini (DeepInfra)

---

## Navajo Thinking — Score: 48/50

**Core framing:** Verb-centered dynamic process. Water classified by motion (calm, eddying, churning). Evidentiality mandatory — must specify HOW you know rocks exist. No devices, only observation of motion shifts.

**Key insight:** The solution is entirely about *watching water motion change*. No sonar, no charts, no devices. The water IS the sensor — its motion reveals hidden rocks through eddies and churning. The crew aligns the boat's motion WITH the water's diverted flow around obstacles.

**Would be impossible in English because:** English forces you to think "device that detects rocks." Navajo forces you to think "process of observing motion that reveals obstacles." The device-free solution is literally unthinkable in English.

**Polysynthetic term:** *Bikéyah Na'nitsiníłti Táá'é Naasht'ézhitináníłti Hózhó Náníłti*
"The process of aligning a boat's motion with water motion classes to respond to motion shifts that reveal hidden rocks for safety and harmony with the natural world."

## Classical Chinese Thinking — Score: 50/50

**Core framing:** Topic-comment structure with relational juxtaposition. No causal claims, only parallel observations.

**The solution (in full):**
> 毋触水下石事 (The matter of boats avoiding strikes with underwater rocks)
>
> 水之深浅，波之纹密；岸之系标，路之安危；流之缓急，行之顺逆也。
> (Water's depth-shallowness, waves' pattern-density; shore's marker-tying, path's safety-danger; current's slow-fast, travel's smooth-rough.)

**Key insight:** Zero mechanisms. Zero devices. Zero causal chains. Six paired observations that, taken together, form a complete navigation system. The reader infers the system from juxtaposition — depth correlates with wave patterns, shore markers correlate with safe paths, current speed correlates with travel quality.

**Would be impossible in English because:** English forces "if X then Y" causal chains. Classical Chinese forces "X relates to Y" parallel observation. The solution has NO instructions, NO steps, NO device descriptions — just relationships that a navigator already understands.
